Do you install countertops near Springfield, MA?

Yes. D&C Granite & Marble serves Ludlow, Springfield, Chicopee, Wilbraham, Longmeadow, East Longmeadow, Palmer, and surrounding Western Massachusetts towns.

Is quartz or granite better?

Quartz is low-maintenance and consistent in appearance. Granite is natural, durable, and unique from slab to slab. D&C helps you choose the right material for your style, use, and budget.

How do I get a countertop quote?

Call the showroom or stop by with rough measurements, photos, or a sketch. D&C can help you compare granite, quartz, marble, remnants, edge profiles, sinks, and backsplash options.
