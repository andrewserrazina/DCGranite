Material options

Choose the right stone for your fireplace.

D&C works with a variety of natural stone materials to create fireplace surrounds that match your home's aesthetic and your personal preferences.

Granite Fireplaces

Granite offers durability and heat resistance perfect for fireplace applications. Natural patterns and colors range from deep blacks and grays to warm browns and dramatic veining. Granite hearths and surrounds stand up to high temperatures and daily use.

Marble Fireplaces

Marble brings timeless elegance and luxury to fireplace designs. Classic white marble with gray veining creates a sophisticated look, while colored marbles offer warmth and character. Marble is ideal for surrounds and mantels in formal living spaces.

Custom Stone Options

D&C also works with other stone materials including limestone, soapstone, and specialty stones. Each material offers unique colors, patterns, and textures. Visit the Ludlow showroom to view available slabs and discuss which stone fits your project.

Design options

Fireplace surround styles and details.

D&C fabricates fireplace surrounds in a variety of styles, from sleek contemporary designs to traditional mantels with decorative details.

Modern Linear Surrounds

Clean lines, minimal ornamentation, and smooth polished finishes for contemporary homes. Often paired with floating stone shelves and streamlined hearths.

Traditional Mantels

Classic fireplace mantels with decorative corbels, columns, and detailed edge profiles. Ideal for Colonial, Victorian, and traditional home styles.

Custom Hearths

Stone hearths fabricated to your fireplace dimensions. Raised or flush hearths available in polished, honed, or textured finishes.

Full Stone Walls

Floor-to-ceiling stone fireplace walls that create dramatic focal points. Combine materials, textures, and finishes for unique designs.
