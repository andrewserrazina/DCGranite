Design Features

Popular Kitchen Island Design Options

From dramatic waterfall edges to integrated seating, we create islands that match your vision and lifestyle.

💧

Waterfall Edges

Stone extends vertically down the sides, creating a continuous "waterfall" from countertop to floor. Modern, dramatic, and protects island edges from damage.

🪑

Integrated Seating

12-15 inch overhang accommodates bar stools for casual dining and entertaining. We ensure proper support with hidden brackets or decorative corbels.

📊

Multi-Level Surfaces

Two-tier design with elevated bar seating or lowered prep area. Create distinct zones for cooking, dining, and entertaining.

🚿

Sink Integration

Precision cutouts for undermount or farmhouse sinks. We coordinate with plumbers for seamless installation and proper drainage.

🔥

Cooktop Cutouts

Custom cutouts for induction or gas cooktops. Heat-resistant stone provides safe, durable cooking surface.

✨

Edge Profiles

Choose from beveled, ogee, bullnose, or custom edge profiles to match your kitchen's style and add distinctive detail.

Common Questions

Kitchen Island FAQs

What materials are best for kitchen island tops? ▼
Granite, quartz, and marble are all excellent for kitchen islands. Granite is heat-resistant and durable with unique patterns. Quartz is non-porous and low-maintenance. Marble offers classic elegance. Your choice depends on your cooking habits, design preferences, and maintenance preferences.
How much does a custom kitchen island cost? ▼
Kitchen island costs vary based on size, material, edge profile, and integrated features like seating or cooktops. Contact us for a free estimate. We'll discuss your vision, take measurements, and provide accurate pricing with no obligation.
Can you create a waterfall edge island? ▼
Yes! Waterfall edges are one of our specialties. We extend stone vertically down the sides for a dramatic, modern look that protects island edges and creates visual continuity from top to bottom.
Do you handle sink and cooktop cutouts? ▼
Absolutely. We create precision cutouts for undermount sinks, farmhouse sinks, and cooktops. We coordinate with plumbers and electricians for seamless installation and proper connections.
How long does it take to create a custom island? ▼
From design consultation to installation, expect 3-4 weeks. Material selection and design takes 1-2 weeks. Fabrication typically takes 7-10 business days. We'll provide a detailed timeline when you visit.
Can you match my existing countertops? ▼
Yes, we can match your existing stone or select complementary materials. Bring a sample of your current countertops to our showroom so we can find coordinating options.
