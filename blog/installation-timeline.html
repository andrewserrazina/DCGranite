Kitchen Countertop Installation: Complete Timeline & What to Expect

Last Updated: May 2026 | 8 min read

You've chosen your granite or quartz countertops—now what? Understanding the installation timeline helps you plan your kitchen remodel, coordinate with other contractors, and know exactly when you'll have a functional kitchen again.

After installing over 1,000 countertops across Western Massachusetts since 1990, we've refined our process to be efficient, predictable, and minimally disruptive to your daily life.

Quick Answer: From slab selection to final installation, expect 2-3 weeks for most kitchen countertop projects. This includes templating (1-3 days after deposit), fabrication (5-10 business days), and installation (3-6 hours).

Complete Installation Timeline

Week 1: Selection & Templating

Day 1-2: Showroom Visit & Slab Selection

  • Duration: 1-2 hours
  • What happens: Visit showroom, select exact slab, choose edge profile
  • Payment: 50% deposit to reserve slab

Day 3-5: Professional Templating

  • Duration: 1-2 hours at your home
  • What happens: Team creates precise templates of cabinets
  • Requirements: Cabinets must be installed, level, and in final position

Week 2: Fabrication

Days 6-15: Stone Fabrication

  • Duration: 5-10 business days
  • What happens: Cut stone to match templates, polish edges, create cutouts
  • Where: Our Ludlow fabrication facility
  • Updates: We'll call when fabrication is complete

Week 3: Installation & Completion

Installation Day

  • Duration: 3-6 hours (depends on kitchen size)
  • What happens: Old counters removed, new counters installed, seams joined
  • Crew size: 2-4 installers
  • Final payment: Due upon completion

Phase-by-Phase Breakdown

Phase 1: Slab Selection & Deposit

Visit our Ludlow showroom to see full slabs in person, compare colors and patterns, select your specific slab, choose edge profile, and sign contract with 50% deposit.

What to Bring:

  • Cabinet door or photo of your cabinetry
  • Paint swatches or kitchen photos
  • Rough measurements of countertop area
  • Budget range
  • Inspiration photos (Pinterest, Houzz)

Timeline: 1-2 hours

Phase 2: Templating

Scheduled 1-3 business days after deposit. Takes 1-2 hours. Requires someone home to answer questions.

What Our Team Does:

  • Measures precisely using laser measurements
  • Creates cardboard or digital templates
  • Notes sink location, cooktop cutouts, outlets
  • Identifies issues (cabinets not level, walls out of square)
  • Discusses seam placement
  • Confirms edge profile and overhang depth

CRITICAL - Cabinets Must Be:

  • Fully installed
  • Level and plumb
  • In their final position
  • Securely fastened to walls
  • No old countertops on top (unless we're removing them)

Phase 3: Fabrication

What Happens in Our Facility:

  • Day 1-2: Template review and cutting plan created
  • Day 3-5: Stone cut using CNC machines (precision within 1/16 inch)
  • Day 6-8: Edges shaped and polished to high shine
  • Day 8-9: Sink and cooktop cutouts created
  • Day 10: Granite sealed, quality control, packaged for transport

Timeline: 5-10 business days | No action required from you

Phase 4: Installation Day

Before Installation (Night Before):

  • Clear countertops completely
  • Remove items from cabinets below
  • Provide clear path for installers
  • Arrange for pets to be secured
  • Park cars to allow installation vehicle access

Installation Process:

  • Hour 1: Remove old countertops, disconnect plumbing, clean cabinet tops
  • Hours 2-3: Position stone pieces, check level, apply adhesive, secure to cabinets
  • Hour 3-4: Join seam pieces, apply color-matched epoxy, polish smooth
  • Hour 4-5: Set sink, install cooktop, apply silicone
  • Hour 5-6: Clean surfaces, remove debris, final quality check

Timeline: 3-6 hours | You need to be home

How to Speed Up Your Installation

1. Have Cabinets Ready

The #1 delay is cabinets not being ready for templating. Coordinate with your cabinet installer to have everything done before we template.

Timeline savings: Eliminates 1-2 week delay

2. Choose In-Stock Materials

Common granite and quartz colors are in stock. Exotic slabs may need to be ordered.

Timeline savings: Eliminates 2-4 week wait for special orders

3. Be Flexible with Scheduling

If you can be flexible on installation day/time, we can fit you in faster.

Timeline savings: 3-7 days

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can you rush my installation?

A: Sometimes. Rush jobs may incur additional fees ($300-800) and depend on current schedule. Quality should never be rushed.

Q: What if I'm not ready by installation day?

A: We'll reschedule, but your project goes back into the queue. Could add 1-2 weeks.

Q: Can I use my kitchen during fabrication?

A: If you still have old counters, yes! If not, you'll need temporary solutions.

Q: How long until I can use my new counters?

A: Immediately for most purposes. Wait 24 hours for seam areas to fully cure before heavy use.

Q: Do you work weekends?

A: Installation can be scheduled on Saturdays for an additional fee. Sundays are typically unavailable.

Pre-Installation Checklist

Before Templating:

  • All cabinets installed
  • Cabinets are level and secure
  • Dishwasher installed (if applicable)
  • Old countertops removed (or scheduled to be removed)
  • Appliances in place
  • Walls painted (to avoid damage later)

Before Installation:

  • Clear all countertops
  • Empty cabinets below counters
  • Disconnect plumbing (or schedule plumber)
  • Arrange for pets to be secured
  • Clear driveway/path for truck
  • Someone home during installation
  • Final payment ready
