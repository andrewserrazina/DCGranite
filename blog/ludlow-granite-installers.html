Why Choose a Local Ludlow Countertop Installer?

1. You See the Actual Slab Before Purchase

Local Advantage: Visit D&C Granite's Ludlow showroom at 664 Center Street and select your exact slab from our 100+ granite, quartz, and marble options. You'll see the real stone—not a 3" sample or photo.

Big-Box Store Experience: Choose from small samples, then hope the slab that arrives matches. Natural stone varies dramatically from slab to slab.

Why It Matters: Granite and marble are natural materials. Color, veining, and pattern vary significantly between slabs. Seeing your actual piece ensures you get exactly what you expect.

2. Faster Turnaround Times

Local Timeline:

  • Templating: Scheduled within 2-4 days of deposit
  • Fabrication: 5-10 business days
  • Installation: Often same week as fabrication completes
  • Total: 2-3 weeks from order to completion

National Chain Timeline:

  • Templating: 1-2 weeks out
  • Fabrication: Slabs may ship to off-site facility
  • Installation: Scheduled when crew is in your area
  • Total: 4-8 weeks (sometimes longer)

Why It Matters: Kitchen remodels are disruptive. The faster you have functional countertops, the faster your life returns to normal.

3. Written Warranty

Minimum Warranty:

  • 1 year on installation workmanship
  • Lifetime on material defects (manufacturer warranty)

Get in Writing:

  • Warranty length
  • What's covered
  • How to file claims

Red Flags: When to Walk Away

🚩 Extremely Low Price

If one quote is 40%+ below others, ask why. Quality granite installation has market rates. Extremely low prices indicate corners being cut.

🚩 No Physical Location

Installers who only do "on-site" consultations, have no showroom, or work from home with no business address are risky. If issues arise, they're hard to find.

🚩 Pressure for Full Payment Upfront

Standard payment terms: 50% deposit at contract signing, 50% due upon installation completion

Red flag: "Pay 100% now for a discount" or "Cash only, no receipt"

🚩 No Written Contract

Every reputable installer provides a written contract including material specifications, edge profile, square footage, total price breakdown, timeline, warranty, and payment schedule.
