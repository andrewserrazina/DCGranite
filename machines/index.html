Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Quick answers to questions we hear most from fabricators.

What's the power requirement?

Standard 220V/3-phase power. Same as most shop equipment. If you don't have 3-phase, we can help you size a phase converter.

How much floor space does it need?

Footprint is 8ft x 6ft. But because it's portable, you can move it anywhere in your shop or even take it on site. You're not dedicating floor space permanently.

What stones can it handle?

Works on granite, marble, and quartz. Handles slabs up to 3cm thick. Adjustable settings for different stone types and finish requirements.

What's included with the machine?

Machine, on-site setup and training (2-4 hours), operating manual, video training access, and lifetime phone support. We'll train your team so you're running jobs the same day.

What's the delivery timeline?

Typically 2-4 weeks from order. Call us to check current availability - we sometimes have units in stock for immediate delivery.

Do you offer warranty?

Yes. 2-year parts warranty included. Lifetime phone support. If something breaks, we fix it fast. Parts shipped next-day. We can't afford downtime either.

What maintenance is required?

Minimal. Grease fittings monthly. Quick visual inspection weekly. We'll show you everything during setup. Takes 5-10 minutes a month.

Can I try it before buying?

Absolutely. We offer free on-site demos within 100 miles. Bring your actual slabs. See results on your stone. No obligation. We come to you.
