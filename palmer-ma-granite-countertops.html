Common Questions

Palmer Countertop FAQs

Do you serve all Palmer neighborhoods? ▼
Yes, D&C Granite serves all Palmer neighborhoods including Palmer Center, Three Rivers, Bondsville, Thorndike, Old Hill, Depot Village, and all other Palmer areas. Our showroom is located in nearby Ludlow.
How long does countertop installation take? ▼
The full process typically takes 2-3 weeks from consultation to installation. This includes material selection, templating visit, fabrication (7-10 business days), and professional installation (usually one day).
What's included in the free estimate? ▼
Our free estimates include showroom or in-home consultation, material selection guidance, accurate measurements, detailed pricing, and project timeline. No obligation required.
What's the difference between granite and quartz? ▼
Granite is natural stone mined from quarries—each slab is unique. Quartz is engineered from natural quartz crystals mixed with resins. Granite is heat-resistant and timeless; quartz is non-porous and low-maintenance. Both are excellent for Palmer kitchens.
Can I see slabs before ordering? ▼
Absolutely! Visit our Ludlow showroom to see our full selection of granite slabs and quartz options in person. We encourage Palmer homeowners to see and touch materials before making decisions.
Do you handle sink installation? ▼
We create precise cutouts for undermount and drop-in sinks as part of our countertop installation. For plumbing connections, we coordinate with your plumber or can recommend trusted local professionals.
