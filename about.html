Craftsmanship you can see in every edge, seam, and finish.

D&C Granite & Marble Design Gallery is a local stone shop built around honest work, careful fabrication, and custom countertops made to fit real homes across Western Massachusetts.

I do for you as I would do for me.

That simple idea is the standard behind D&C Granite & Marble Design Gallery. Owner and master craftsman Luis Serrazina began learning stonework at age 13, building decades of hands-on experience before creating a business focused on quality, trust, and personal attention.

For customers, that means the work is never treated like a generic install. Every countertop, vanity, fireplace, and custom stone piece is approached with the goal of making it look right, fit right, and last.
