Material Options

Choose Your Bathroom Vanity Material

Each material offers unique benefits for bathroom applications. Our team helps you select the perfect option for durability, maintenance, and aesthetic.

🪨

Granite Vanities

Timeless Beauty: Natural granite creates stunning visual interest with unique patterns and colors.

Durability: Granite is extremely durable and resists scratches and impacts—perfect for active bathrooms.

Heat & Stain Resistant: Sealed granite handles hair tools, cosmetics, and daily bathroom use with ease.

✨

Quartz Vanities

Low Maintenance: Non-porous surface never needs sealing. Resists stains, bacteria, and moisture—ideal for bathrooms.

Consistent Appearance: Engineered for uniform color and pattern. Perfect for matching existing bathroom design.

Hygienic: Non-porous nature prevents bacterial growth and mold. Simple soap and water cleaning.

💎

Marble Vanities

Timeless Elegance: Classic marble brings luxury and sophistication to any bathroom design.

Sophisticated Veining: Dramatic natural veining creates stunning one-of-a-kind vanities.

Cool Surface: Natural coolness of marble adds to spa-like bathroom experience.

Common Questions

Bathroom Remodeling FAQs

What materials are best for bathroom vanities? ▼
Granite, quartz, and marble are all excellent for bathroom vanities. Granite offers natural beauty and durability. Quartz is non-porous and requires minimal maintenance. Marble provides timeless elegance but requires more care. Your choice depends on your design preferences and maintenance tolerance.
How long does a bathroom remodel take? ▼
A typical bathroom remodel with custom stone vanities takes 3-4 weeks from initial consultation to completion. This includes design, material selection, precise templating, fabrication (7-10 business days), and professional installation.
Can you create a double vanity? ▼
Yes! We specialize in custom double vanities in granite, quartz, and marble. We can create matching pieces or contrasting materials, with dual sinks, integrated lighting, and custom storage solutions that fit your bathroom perfectly.
Do you handle undermount or drop-in sinks? ▼
We create precision cutouts for both undermount and drop-in sinks. Undermount sinks offer a seamless look, while drop-in sinks are more traditional. We coordinate with plumbers to ensure perfect installation and drainage.
What about tub surrounds and shower enclosures? ▼
We create complete bathroom stonework including tub surrounds, shower enclosures, and bathroom walls in granite, marble, or quartz. Waterproof sealing ensures long-lasting protection in wet bathroom environments.
How much does a bathroom remodel cost? ▼
Bathroom remodel costs vary based on vanity size, material choice, configuration, and features. Contact us for a free estimate. We'll discuss your vision, take measurements, and provide accurate pricing with no obligation.
